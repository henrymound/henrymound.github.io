Photon

Light Board Test Demo from Current Lights on Vimeo.

Photon: Wireless Demo from Current Lights on Vimeo.

Photon was a project that enabled control of an LED lamp from anywhere in the world. The light itself consisted of 66 PWM RGB LEDs connected to an electric imp. I developed an iPhone and OSX companion apps to control the lamp via the electric imp's WiFi connectivity.
